Candace Ho is a Taiwanese American filmmaker based in Los Angeles. Her work has screened at festivals across the country, including Tribeca Film Festival, Los Angeles Asian Pacific Film Festival, and San Diego Asian Film Festival.

Candace is currently writing a pilot based on her most recent short, Sorry Grandpa Hsiao, which was created for the 2023 Rising Voices program and premiered at Tribeca.
