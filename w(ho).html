W(HO)

writer, director, editor, & producer

released in 2022

5 mins | doc (autobiographical, comedy) | mixed media

Growing up with the last name Ho, jokes came with the territory. And naturally, shame and resentment towards the word grew as a kid. w(HO) explores the growing pains of a young woman through five different eras of varying relationships with the word.

Created for the 2021 class of Visual Communications's Armed with a Camera (AWC) Fellowship. Premiered opening night at the 2022 Los Angeles Asian Pacific Film Festival.
